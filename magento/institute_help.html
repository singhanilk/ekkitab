Steps to register your school/college/institute

  1. Please enter the required Insitute information, the mandatory fields are marked with an * at the end. Please provide the Telephone number with STD code
  2. If you wish users to donate any book to your library in addition to your wishlist, please check "Allow customers to donate any book to your library".
  3. For Administrator Details: If you are already an user of the system and wish to add your institute, please check the option, you will be only asked for the login user name and password On submitting the same, the institute will be added and you will be logged in.
  4. If you are not an existing user, on providing the First name, Last Name, Valid Email address and password, you will be registered as an user and your instiute will be added also.

Confirmation Email

  1. After you have submitted the insitute details, an confirmation email will be sent out to the administrator email address, notifying that your institute has been added.

Institute Validation

  1. After Ekkitab validates the institute details which normally takes upto 12 hours, it will be displayed on the Ekkitab network.
