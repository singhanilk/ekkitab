Steps to register your school/college/institute y 1. Please provide the Insitute infomration, the mandatory fields are marked with an * at the end 2. If you wish users to donate any book to your library in addition to your wishlist, please check "Allow customers to donate any book to your library". 3. An administrator of the school, will be able to add books to the school wishlist and will receive all communication from Ekkitab.ou 4. If you are already an user of the system and wish to add your institute, please check the option, you will be only asked for the login user name and password On submitting the same, the institute will be added and you will be logged in. 5. If you are not an existing user, on providing the First name, Last Name, Valid Email address and password, you will be registered as an user and your instiute will be added also. 6. An confirmation email will be sent out to the administrator email address, notifying that your institute has been added. 7. After Ekkitab validates the institute details, it will be displayed on the Ekkitab network . This normally takes upto 24 hrs.
